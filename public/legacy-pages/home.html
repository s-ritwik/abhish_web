Latest Happenings

Sabal 20 is an unmanned logistic drone with 20 kg payload lifting capacity. It was recently inducted into Indian Army. The original design study was carried out in the lab at IIT Kanpur and subsequently manufactured and tested by our startup EndureAir Systems Pvt. Ltd. See a news clip below. The UAV is a rotary wing UAS with multirole capability, offers unprecedented payload fraction of 0.33 and is able to operate at altitudes as high as 6000 m.
