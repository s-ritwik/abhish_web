  • Class of 1979 Research Fellowship, 2017.
  • Lead the team that won First and second rounds of 2018 UAS Flight and Payload Challenge organized by National Institute of Standards and Technology (US Department of Commerce) and Public Safety Communications Innovation Accelerator (USA).
